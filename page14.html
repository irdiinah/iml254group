Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ)

How do I make a booking?

You can make a booking through our website, by phone, WhatsApp, or by visiting Whisker Mansion. We recommended booking in advance to secure your prefered date and time.

Can I cancel or reschedule my appoinment?

Yes! Please inform us at least 24 hours in advance if you need to cancel or reschedule your appointment.

Does my pet need to be  vaccinated?

Yes. For the safety of all pets, we require all guests to have up-to-date vaccinations.

Should I feed my pet before grooming?

A light meal is recommended, but avoid feeding your pet immediately before the grooming appointment.

What should I bring for my pet's hotel stay?

Please bring your pet's food, medication (if needed), favorite toy or blanket, and a copy of their vaccination record.

What happens if my pet becomes unwell??

If your pet becomes ill or injured, we will contact you immediately and seek veterinary assistance if necessary.
